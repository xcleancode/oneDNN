dnnl_alg_kind_t dnnl_rnn_desc_t::cell_kind

RNN cell kind.

Must be one of dnnl_vanilla_rnn, dnnl_vanilla_lstm, dnnl_vanilla_gru, or dnnl_lbr_gru.
